Different Styles/Aesthetics of 2022

Recently with the new age of socail media, comes new styles for a new generation.Especially with apps such as TikTok, where dressing in your style that makes you feel comfortable is very important to teens now. Fashion is definetely a place of expression, and it's amazing now that we can all share this interest with others,and find others with a similar sens eof style to our own!

But if you're still unsure about your own aesthetic, you can quickly take a look at each type down below and see which styles you gravitate towards.

Subersive Aesthetics

In her viral TikTok post, Panzoni defined subersive basic as "basic that rebel up to the point of them losing their utility." By reforming a basic article of clothing like a white t-shirt into a shirt with holes, and playing with imbalanced symmetry, the shirt is rebelling against it's original form and intent

Bohemian Aesthetics

Bohemian style, also often called boho chic or boho fashion is a style of dressing that was made popular by the flower children or "hippies" in the 1960's and 1970's.Pertaining to homes/rooms, Bohemian decor is for those who want their home full of lifw, culture,and iteresting items for all the wolrd to see. This aesthetics files in the face of modern sensibilities and embraces creative, carefree and unconventional decor.

Streetwear Aesthetics

Streetwear is a casual fashion style that first became popular in the 1990's.It incoporates comfortable yet trendy clothing such as graphic tees,hoodies,sweatpants and expensive sneakers. Streetwear takes inspiration from both hip-hop culture and skater style,with the added element of intentional product scarcity

Y2K Aesthetics

Y2K (also known as Kaybug) is an aesthetic that was prevalent in popular culture form roughly 1995 to 2004. named after the Y2K Bug, it is characterized by a distinct aesthetic period, encapsulating fashion, hardware design,music and furnishings shining with tech optimism- sometimes literally.

Luxurious Aesthetics

The word luxurious characterized by opulence, sumptuousness, or rich abundance: of relating to, or maerked by luxury.Hence, this aesthetic is most often associated with celebrities and wealthy business owners and their heirs. Boujee people are concerned with wealth,status,and all thing luxury.They'll often show off, or "flex", their wealth on socail media to gain notoriety and followers, or "clout".

Grunge Aesthetics

It's likely a few things come to mind when you think of grunge fashion: namely, distressed denim and baggy silhouettes.And though they may seem difficult to pull off at first, you'll soon realize that there's more than one way to create an angsty ensemble, and that there's even a formula to it.By definition, grunge is all about de-emphasizing the silhouette of the body and looking "untidy" in an attempt to mirror the cool look of popular musicians in both punk and heavy metal rock bands.Like other popular trends, this one dates back as early as the 80's and has been a core aesthetic ever since.

Dark Academia

Originally based on European culture, dark academia style revolves around classic literature, with a focus on history and self discovery. Often times, this aesthetic is more mysterious and "dark" compared to other academia styles.
